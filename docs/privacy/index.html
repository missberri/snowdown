Snowdown Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Snowdown (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Snowdown mobile application and related services (the “App”).

Information We Do Not Collect

Snowdown is designed to provide information about events, schedules, and activities. We do not collect, store, or process personal information.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not include forms, user accounts, or login features
  • We do not track users across apps or websites

Automatically Collected Information

Snowdown does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies that collect personal or device-level data.

Standard, non-identifiable technical information (such as basic web requests required to display content) may be processed by underlying hosting or platform providers, but Snowdown does not access or store this information.

Third-Party Services

The App may display content such as maps, images, or links that originate from third-party services. These services operate independently and have their own privacy practices. Snowdown does not control or receive user data from these services.

Children’s Privacy

Snowdown does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Because Snowdown does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information maintained or secured by the App.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this location with an updated effective date.
